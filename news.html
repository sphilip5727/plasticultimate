World Masters · Nottingham 4 July 2026

USA dominate the 2026 World Masters Club Championships — and Clapham make history at home

The WFDF 2026 World Masters Ultimate Club Championships wrapped up in Nottingham, with the United States claiming seven of the nine world titles on offer. On home soil, Great Britain's Clapham Masters won the Masters Open crown for the very first time, downing reigning champions Boneyard in front of a roaring local crowd — with Canada also celebrating gold on finals weekend.

First world champions crowned in Nottingham

Finals weekend opened with the Great Grand Masters titles: Canada's Vintage edged Nor'Easter 12–11 for women's gold, while Surly (15–9) and Endless Sunset (15–8) sealed divisions for the USA.
