Eligibility

Who is the academy for?

The academy is not locked by ability but instead open to players who feel they align with the 3 Pillars.

01

Open Mindset

Growth in Ultimate comes from embracing what feels uncomfortable, recognising that mastery of the fundamentals is built through challenge, repetition, and reflection.

02

Commitment to Personal Development

Give your best effort, being present, and taking ownership of your progress both on and off the field.

03

Competitive sportsmanship

You want to win — but you play the right way. Spirit of the Game is non-negotiable.
